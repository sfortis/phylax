Major release focused on notification reliability and live system visibility.

New
* CPU and GPU badges in the top bar with a full stats panel on tap.
* Connection status dialog with live latency graph.
* Notifications deep-link by severity: alerts to Review, detections to Explore.
* One notification per event, regardless of Frigate's update frames.

Reliability
* Foreground service promoted to SPECIAL_USE to avoid Android 14+ 6-hour kill.
* WakeLock, WifiLock and WorkManager watchdog survive doze and OEM kills.
* Persistent notification repost-on-dismiss receiver.
* Force-reconnect on network regain.

Fixes
* URLs without a scheme no longer crash.
* Notification camera picker summary now matches the actual filter state.
* Update check failures show a proper error instead of "No updates available".
