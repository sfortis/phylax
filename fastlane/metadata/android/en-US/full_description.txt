Android client for the self-hosted <a href="https://frigate.video">Frigate NVR</a>. Unofficial and not affiliated with the Frigate project.

* Auto URL switching between internal (LAN) and external Frigate based on Wi-Fi SSID.
* Background notifications with snapshot thumbnails. Alerts open the Review timeline; detections open the Explore view.
* Per-camera, per-zone and severity filters. One notification per tracked event.
* CPU and GPU badges in the top bar. Tap for RAM, uptime, per-detector inference and per-camera FPS.
* Connection status dialog with a live latency graph.
* mTLS client certificate support.
* Two-way talk on doorbell and intercom cameras.
* Clip and snapshot downloads.
* Reliable on Android 14+ with SPECIAL_USE foreground service, wake locks and a WorkManager watchdog.

No analytics, no cloud, no account. Credentials kept in encrypted local storage.

Compatible with Frigate 0.12+ for notifications, 0.13 to 0.17 for stats.
